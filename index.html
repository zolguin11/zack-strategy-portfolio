Point of View

Most brands don't have a story problem. They have a translation problem.

They know what they stand for. The messaging is there. The values are there. It sounds right in a room full of people who already understand it.

Then it leaves that room and lands in someone else's world, and nobody cares. Not because it's wrong. Because it doesn't matter to them.
