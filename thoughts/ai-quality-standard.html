Thought experiment

AI is not a shortcut if the standard stays high.

The difference between lazy AI use and valuable AI use is whether the human raises the bar or quietly lowers it.

Place for workflow diagram, prompt loop, or quality rubric visual

I do not think the best AI users are the people who generate the most outputs. I think they are the people who keep a strong internal quality standard while using AI to move through exploration faster.

AI can produce language, options, summaries, structures, and first-pass patterns. But judgment still shows up in what you ask, what you reject, what you combine, what you verify, and what you decide is not good enough yet.

Used well, AI does not replace strategic taste. It gives taste more material to work with.
